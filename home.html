Magic Cake

Magic Cake is one of the tasties cakes the world have tried it and live to tell about it, and these are their storie

We also need to stress that whatever happens to you as a reult of consuming Magic Cake is your responsability. You've been warned, and you can view or legal page for more information




Ingredients


  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter



Directions


  1. Mix a eggs, sugar and butter in a large bowl.
  2. Spread into a non-stick dish
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
  4. Let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes
  5. Eat and enjoy!
